Detailed Itinerary

Hour-by-hour breakdown of your adventure

6 AM

Departure from Your Location

Early morning pickup from your hotel or designated meeting point in Goa.

  • Comfortable AC vehicle transfer
  • Pack essentials: water, snacks, swimwear, towel
8 AM

Arrival at Collem (Dudhsagar Base)

Arrive at the base point for Dudhsagar Waterfalls access.

  • Arrange jeep safari to waterfalls
  • 45-minute thrilling jungle ride
9 AM

Dudhsagar Waterfalls

Experience the majestic 310-meter tall waterfalls.

  • Photography opportunities
  • Safe swimming areas (conditions permitting)
  • 1.5-2 hours at the falls
11:30 AM

Return Jeep Safari

Scenic ride back through Bhagwan Mahaveer Wildlife Sanctuary.

  • Spot wildlife in natural habitat
12:30 PM

Drive to Spice Plantation

30-40 minute transfer to a renowned spice plantation.

1 PM

Traditional Goan Lunch

Buffet lunch featuring local specialties at the plantation.

  • Fish curry rice, chicken xacuti
  • Vegetarian options available
  • Herbal teas & fresh juices
2 PM

Spice Plantation Tour

Guided exploration of the plantation's spice cultivation.

  • See cardamom, pepper, nutmeg, vanilla
  • Betel nut palms & cashew trees
  • Sample fresh spices & fruits
  • Duration: 1-1.5 hours
3:30 PM

Return Journey

Depart for your original pickup location.

  • Comfortable return transfer
  • Estimated arrival by 5:30-6 PM
