Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Himalayan experience

1

Arrival in Bagdogra & Transfer to Darjeeling

Board your morning flight from Goa. Upon arrival at Bagdogra Airport, meet our representative and transfer to Darjeeling (approx. 3-4 hours drive).

  • Flight from Goa to Bagdogra included
  • Comfortable transfer to Darjeeling
  • Check-in at hotel and freshen up
  • Free evening to explore local markets

Overnight stay at Darjeeling hotel.

2

Darjeeling Sightseeing

Full day exploring the Queen of the Hills:

  • Early morning visit to Tiger Hill (4 AM) for sunrise views
  • Ghoom Monastery & Batasia Loop
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Japanese Temple & Peace Pagoda
  • Himalayan Zoological Park & Mountaineering Institute
  • Tea Garden visit & Rangeet Valley View Point

Return to hotel for overnight stay.

3

Darjeeling to Pelling

After breakfast, check out and drive to Pelling (approx. 4-5 hours).

  • Scenic drive through Himalayan landscapes
  • Check-in at Pelling hotel
  • Evening games and activities

Overnight stay at Pelling.

4

Pelling Sightseeing

Full day exploring Pelling's natural and cultural wonders:

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Darap Village visit
  • Rimbi & Kanchenjunga Waterfalls
  • Pemayangtse Monastery
  • Rabdentse Ruins exploration
  • Sky Walk & Chenrezig Statue

Return to hotel for overnight stay.

5

Pelling to Gangtok

After breakfast, check out and drive to Gangtok (approx. 4-5 hours).

  • Check-in at Gangtok hotel
  • Half-day Gangtok sightseeing:
  • Ganesh Tok, Flower Show, Lhasa Falls
  • Tashi View Point & Gonjang Monastery
  • Bakthang Falls & Plant Conservatory
  • Evening free for shopping at MG Marg

Overnight stay at Gangtok.

6

Gangtok Excursion

Full day excursion to high-altitude wonders:

  • Early breakfast at hotel
  • Tsomgo Lake visit (12,400 ft)
  • Baba Mandir exploration
  • Nathula Pass (subject to permit availability)
  • Optional evening shopping at MG Marg

Return to hotel for overnight stay.

7

Departure from Gangtok

After breakfast, check out from hotel.

  • Transfer to Bagdogra Airport (approx. 4-5 hours)
  • Flight back to Goa
  • Return with unforgettable Himalayan memories

Tour concludes with wonderful memories of the Eastern Himalayas!
