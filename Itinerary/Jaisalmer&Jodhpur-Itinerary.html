Tour Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your desert adventure

1

Arrival in Jodhpur

Arrive at Jodhpur Airport at 3 PM. Transfer to your hotel and check-in.

Airport pickup included
Check-in at hotel and freshen up
Visit Umaid Bhawan Palace Museum
Evening stroll at local markets

Overnight stay at Jodhpur hotel.

2

Jodhpur Sightseeing

Full day exploring the Blue City.

Breakfast at hotel
Explore Mehrangarh Fort, one of the largest forts in India
Visit Jaswant Thada, a stunning marble cenotaph
Head to Mandore Gardens
Shopping at Clock Tower Market

Return to hotel for overnight stay.

3

Jodhpur to Jaisalmer

After breakfast, check out and drive to Jaisalmer (approx. 5-6 hours).

Scenic drive through Thar Desert landscapes
Check-in at Jaisalmer hotel
Explore Patwon Ki Haveli
Visit Gadisar Lake
Evening stroll around Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Quila)

Overnight stay at Jaisalmer.

4

Jaisalmer to Sam Sand Dunes

Experience the magic of the Thar Desert.

Breakfast at hotel
Explore Salim Singh Ki Haveli
Visit War Museum
Drive to Sam Sand Dunes (about 45 minutes)
Enjoy camel safari in the desert
Cultural performances and traditional Rajasthani dinner at desert camp

Overnight stay at desert camp in Sam Sand Dunes.

5

Departure from Jaisalmer

Early breakfast and drive to Jaisalmer Airport for your 11 AM departure.

Transfer to Jaisalmer Airport
Flight to your next destination
Return with unforgettable memories of Rajasthan

Tour concludes with wonderful memories of Rajasthan's royal heritage!
