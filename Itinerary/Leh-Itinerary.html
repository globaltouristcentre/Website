Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Ladakh experience

1

Arrival in Leh (3,500m)

Arrive at Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport, Leh. Transfer to your hotel for rest and acclimatization.

  • Airport pickup included
  • Check-in at hotel and rest for acclimatization
  • Acclimatization is essential for high-altitude travel
  • If health permits, visit Leh Market and Shanti Stupa for sunset views

Overnight stay in Leh.

2

Leh Local Sightseeing

Full day exploring Leh's key attractions:

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Shanti Stupa - beautiful white-domed Buddhist stupa
  • Leh Palace - historic palace with panoramic views
  • Phyang Monastery - known for serene ambiance
  • Sangam - confluence of Zanskar and Indus Rivers
  • Magnetic Hill - experience the gravity-defying phenomenon
  • Gurudwara Pathar Sahib - revered Sikh shrine

Return to hotel for overnight stay.

3

Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La (5,359m)

Depart by 8:00 AM, driving through the famous Khardung La Pass, one of the highest motorable roads in the world.

  • Scenic drive through Khardung La Pass
  • Arrive at Nubra Valley and check in at homestay/camp
  • Visit Diskit Monastery with 32m Maitreya Buddha statue
  • Explore Hunder Sand Dunes - stunning cold desert landscape
  • Optional: Enjoy camel ride on double-humped Bactrian camels

Overnight stay in Nubra Valley.

4

Nubra Valley to Turtuk (Northernmost Village)

Start early at 7:30 AM for scenic drive along Shyok River to Turtuk, the northernmost village of India.

  • Explore Baltistani culture and unique heritage
  • Visit historic Turtuk Mosque
  • Explore Turtuk Monastery
  • Breathtaking viewpoint with panoramic vistas
  • Return to Nubra by late afternoon

Overnight stay in Nubra Valley (Hunder).

5

Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake via Shyok River

After breakfast, embark on picturesque drive to Pangong Lake via scenic Shyok River route.

  • Scenic drive through Shyok River valley
  • Spend afternoon by stunning Pangong Tso
  • Marvel at the lake's changing hues (blue to emerald green)
  • Overnight in camps by the lake

Overnight stay in Pangong Lake camps.

6

Pangong Lake to Leh via Chang La Pass

Begin journey back to Leh, crossing Chang La Pass (3rd highest motorable road in the world).

  • Cross Chang La Pass (5,360m)
  • Visit Thiksey Monastery - largest in Ladakh
  • Arrive in Leh by evening
  • Optional evening shopping in Leh market

Overnight stay in Leh.

7

Departure from Leh

After breakfast, transfer to Leh Airport for your onward journey.

  • Airport transfer included
  • Flight to your next destination
  • Cherished memories of Ladakh's unparalleled beauty

Tour concludes with unforgettable Himalayan memories!
