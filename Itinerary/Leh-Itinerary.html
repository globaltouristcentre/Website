Tour Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Ladakh experience

1

Arrival in Leh (3,500m)

Begin your Ladakh adventure with arrival in Leh.

Flight arrival at Leh Airport
Transfer to hotel for check-in
Rest for acclimatization
2

Leh Local Sightseeing

Explore Leh's key attractions and cultural sites.

Breakfast at hotel
Shanti Stupa visit
Leh Palace exploration
Phyang Monastery visit
3

Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La (5,359m)

Drive through one of the highest motorable roads in the world.

Breakfast at hotel
Scenic drive through Khardung La Pass
Check-in at Nubra Valley homestay/camp
Explore Hunder Sand Dunes
4

Nubra Valley to Turtuk (Northernmost Village)

Explore the unique Baltistani culture in India's northernmost village.

Breakfast at accommodation
Scenic drive along Shyok River
Explore Baltistani culture
Visit historic Turtuk Mosque
5

Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake via Shyok River

Journey to the stunning Pangong Tso lake.

Breakfast at accommodation
Scenic drive through Shyok River valley
Pangong Lake exploration
Overnight in lakeside camps
6

Pangong Lake to Leh via Chang La Pass

Return journey crossing the world's 3rd highest motorable road.

Breakfast at camp
Cross Chang La Pass (5,360m)
Visit Thiksey Monastery
Return to Leh hotel
7

Departure from Leh

Return home with unforgettable memories of Ladakh.

Flight to your next destination
Fond memories of Ladakh

Tour Details

What's included and what's not

Inclusions

  • 4-star accommodation in double rooms
  • 6-night stay in comfortable hotels/camps
  • Meals on MAP plan (breakfast and dinner)
  • Private car for all transport and sightseeing
  • Airport pick-up and drop-off services
  • Protected area permits (Inner Line Permit)
  • 1 oxygen cylinder in car for emergencies
  • Ladakh environment fee and applicable taxes
  • Parking fees and toll taxes

Exclusions

  • Lunch during the trip
  • Personal expenses (shopping, tips, etc.)
  • Any additional activities not mentioned
  • Medical expenses (if any)
  • Anything not mentioned in inclusions
