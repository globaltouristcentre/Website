Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Aurangabad experience

1

Mumbai to Aurangabad

Depart from Mumbai to Aurangabad via car.

  • Scenic drive to Aurangabad
  • Arrive in Aurangabad and check in to hotel
  • Relax and have lunch
  • Visit Bibi Ka Maqbara - stunning Mughal-era monument
  • Explore Panchakki - historical water mill

Overnight stay in Aurangabad.

2

Ajanta Caves Excursion

Full day excursion to the magnificent Ajanta Caves.

  • Early breakfast at hotel
  • Depart for Ajanta Caves (approx. 2-hour drive)
  • Explore 30 rock-cut Buddhist caves with intricate frescoes
  • Lunch at nearby restaurant
  • Return to Aurangabad for rest

Overnight stay in Aurangabad.

3

Ellora Caves & Aurangabad Sightseeing

Explore the wonders of Ellora Caves and more.

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit Ellora Caves (UNESCO World Heritage Site, 40-min drive)
  • Explore Kailasa Temple (Cave 16) - marvel of architecture
  • Visit Daulatabad Fort (Devagiri Fort) for panoramic views
  • Evening free for local markets or Hyderabadi cuisine

Overnight stay in Aurangabad.

4

Aurangabad to Mumbai

Check out and return to Mumbai with sightseeing en route.

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit Aurangabad Caves - 12 Buddhist caves with carvings
  • Relaxed lunch
  • Proceed to Mumbai with sightseeing en route:
  • Gateway of India
  • Taj Hotel
  • Marine Drive

Tour concludes with wonderful historical memories!
