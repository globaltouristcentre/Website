Detailed Itinerary

Hour-by-hour breakdown of your nature adventure

07:00 AM

Departure from Your Location

Begin your journey early to maximize the day. Our representative will pick you up from your hotel or designated meeting point in South Goa.

  • Comfortable AC vehicle transfer
  • Pack water, snacks, comfortable shoes
09:00 AM

Arrival at Netravali Wildlife Sanctuary

Explore the sanctuary famous for its rich biodiversity. Spot rare birds, butterflies, and plant species.

  • Optional local guide (additional cost)
  • Photography opportunities
10:30 AM

Visit to Bubbling Lake (Budbudyanchi Tail)

Witness the fascinating phenomenon where bubbles emerge from the lake's surface when you clap or make noise.

  • Unique natural phenomenon
  • Duration: 30 minutes to 1 hour
  • Nearby Gopinath Temple visit
11:30 AM

Trek to Netravali Waterfalls

Embark on an easy-to-moderate trek through dense forests and rocky trails to reach the pristine waterfalls.

  • Scenic forest trek
  • Refreshing dip in the falls (weather permitting)
  • Duration: 2-3 hours total
02:00 PM

Lunch Break

Enjoy a delicious Goan meal at a local restaurant or homestay.

  • Traditional Goan dishes available
  • Fish curry rice or vegetarian options
  • Local desserts
03:30 PM

Explore Netravali's Scenic Villages

Drive through charming villages with lush greenery, paddy fields, and traditional Goan houses.

  • Traditional Goan architecture
  • Optional interaction with locals
04:30 PM

Visit a Spice Plantation (Optional)

Learn about spice cultivation like pepper, cardamom, and vanilla with a guided plantation tour.

  • Spice demonstration
  • Option to purchase fresh spices
  • Additional cost applies
05:30 PM

Return Journey

Begin your return journey, enjoying the scenic drive through Goa's countryside.

  • Comfortable return transfer
  • Estimated arrival back by 7:30 PM
