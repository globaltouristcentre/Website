Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Kerala experience

1

Arrival in Trivandrum

Begin your Kerala adventure with arrival in Trivandrum.

  • Flight arrival at Trivandrum Airport
  • Transfer to hotel for check-in
  • Overnight stay in Trivandrum
2

Trivandrum - Kovalam

Explore Trivandrum's rich culture before heading to beachside Kovalam.

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple (richest temple in India)
  • Jatayu Earth Center (world's largest bird sculpture)
  • Azhimala Shiva Statue & beach visit
  • Check-in at Kovalam hotel
  • Evening at leisure in Kovalam
3

Kovalam - Alleppey (Houseboat)

Experience Kerala's famous backwaters with an overnight houseboat stay.

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Drive to Alleppey (approx. 3 hours)
  • Board traditional Kerala houseboat
  • Lunch, evening snacks & dinner onboard
  • Cruise through scenic backwaters
  • Overnight on houseboat
4

Alleppey - Suryanelli (Camping)

From backwaters to tea plantations for a unique camping experience.

  • Breakfast on houseboat
  • Drive to Suryanelli via waterfalls
  • Visit Cheeyappara & Valara Waterfalls
  • Check-in at tea plantation campsite
  • Welcome drink on arrival
  • Optional Phantom Hill exploration
  • Campfire & dinner under the stars
5

Suryanelli - Munnar

Sunrise jeep safari followed by exploration of Munnar's tea country.

  • Early morning jeep safari to Kolukkumalai (04:30 AM)
  • Sunrise at highest tea plantation
  • Breakfast at campsite (08:30 AM)
  • Drive to Munnar
  • Visit Mattupetty Dam & Echo Point
  • Scenic Gap Road drive
  • Botanical garden visit
  • Kathakali dance performance
  • Check-in at Munnar hotel
6

Munnar - Kochi - Departure

Final day exploring Munnar before return journey.

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Drive to Kochi (approx. 4 hours)
  • Kochi sightseeing (time permitting)
  • Drop at Ernakulam Railway Station by 8 PM
  • Overnight train journey back to Goa
7

Arrival in Goa

Return home with unforgettable memories of Kerala.

  • Arrival in Goa by 11:50 AM
  • Fond memories of Kerala's backwaters and tea gardens
