Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Andaman experience

1

Arrival in Port Blair

Upon arrival at Port Blair airport, our representative will greet you and accompany you to your hotel for check-in.

  • Airport pickup included
  • Check-in at hotel and freshen up
  • Visit Cellular Jail (Kala Pani) - historic colonial-era prison
  • Relax at Corbyn's Cove Beach
  • Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail in evening

Overnight stay in Port Blair.

2

Baratang Island Excursion

Embark on a scenic road trip from Port Blair to Baratang Island (approx. 100 km).

  • Travel through dense tropical forest and Jarawa Tribal Reserve
  • Fiber boat ride through fascinating mangroves
  • Trek to Limestone Caves (natural formations)
  • Return to Port Blair by evening

Overnight stay in Port Blair.

3

Port Blair to Havelock Island

Take an early morning ferry from Port Blair to Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep).

  • Ferry ride (1.5-2.5 hours depending on service)
  • Check-in at hotel/resort (early check-in available)
  • Visit Radhanagar Beach (one of Asia's most beautiful beaches)
  • Sunset viewing at Radhanagar Beach

Overnight stay in Havelock Island.

4

Havelock Island - Elephant Beach

Full day beach and water activities at Elephant Beach.

  • Breakfast at resort
  • 25-minute boat ride to Elephant Beach
  • Complimentary snorkeling in crystal-clear waters
  • Sunbathing and relaxation on the beach
  • Note: Subject to weather conditions

Overnight stay in Havelock Island.

5

Havelock to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep)

After breakfast, check out and take ferry to Neil Island.

  • Scenic ferry ride to Neil Island
  • Check-in at hotel
  • Visit Bharatpur Beach for snorkeling
  • Explore Natural Rock Formation
  • Sunset at Laxmanpur Beach

Overnight stay in Neil Island.

6

Neil Island to Port Blair

After breakfast, check out and return to Port Blair.

  • Scenic ferry ride back to Port Blair
  • Check-in at hotel
  • Free time for shopping or relaxation

Overnight stay in Port Blair.

7

Departure from Port Blair

After breakfast, check-out from hotel.

  • Transfer to Port Blair airport
  • Flight to your next destination
  • Fond memories of your Andaman adventure

Tour concludes with wonderful beach memories!
