Tour Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Hampi experience

1

Arrival at Hospete & Hampi Sightseeing

Arrive at Hospete Station and meet your pre-arranged car and driver. Check in to your hotel in Hospete.

Arrival at Hospete Station
Check-in at Hospete hotel
Lunch at local restaurant
Explore Sacred Hampi Complex
Virupaksha Temple (spiritual heart of Hampi)
Hampi Bazaar ruins
Hemakuta Hill Temples (panoramic views)
2

Full-Day Hampi Exploration

After breakfast, full day exploring Hampi's architectural treasures.

Breakfast at hotel
Vittala Temple Complex (iconic Stone Chariot)
King's Balance ruins
Coracle boat ride on Tungabhadra River
Lunch at riverside café
Riverside ruins & temples
Anjaneya Hill (Hanuman Temple)
Evening at Tungabhadra Dam (optional boating)
3

Sanapur Lake & Departure

Morning after checkout, visit Sanapur Lake before departure.

Breakfast at hotel
Sanapur Lake (optional coracle ride)
Final visit to Vittala Temple
Lunch at local restaurant
Departure for return journey
