Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Rajasthan experience

1

Arrival in Jaipur

Arrive at Jaipur at night and check in to your hotel. Relax after your journey.

  • Check-in at Jaipur hotel
  • Overnight stay in Jaipur
2

Jaipur Sightseeing

Full day exploring the Pink City's iconic landmarks:

  • Amber Fort with elephant ride option
  • City Palace and Hawa Mahal
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Albert Hall Museum
  • Photo stop at Jal Mahal
  • Explore local markets in evening

Overnight stay at Jaipur hotel.

3

Jaipur to Udaipur via Nathdwara

Morning sightseeing in Jaipur before departing for Udaipur:

  • Visit Jantar Mantar observatory
  • Quick shopping time
  • Drive to Udaipur with stop at Nathdwara
  • Visit Shiva Statue (Statue of Belief)
  • Check-in at Udaipur hotel

Overnight stay in Udaipur.

4

Udaipur Sightseeing

Full day exploring the City of Lakes:

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • City Palace complex
  • Boat ride on Lake Pichola (Jag Mandir view)
  • Saheliyon Ki Bari gardens
  • Karni Mata Temple for sunset views
  • Sajjangarh Fort if time permits

Overnight stay in Udaipur.

5

Departure from Udaipur

After breakfast, check out from hotel.

  • Transfer to Udaipur Airport
  • Flight back home
  • Return with royal memories of Rajasthan

Tour concludes with wonderful memories of Rajasthan's heritage!
