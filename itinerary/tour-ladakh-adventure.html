6 Nights 7 Days - Leh, Turtuk, Pangong Tour

Experience the majestic landscapes of Ladakh

7 Days / 6 Nights Leh, Nubra Valley, Pangong Lake

Trip Itinerary

7 Days / 6 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Leh

  • Arrive at Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport, Leh (Altitude: 3,500m)
  • Transfer to your hotel for rest and acclimatization
  • If health permits, visit the Leh Market and Shanti Stupa to enjoy a serene sunset view
  • Overnight stay in Leh

Day 2: Leh Local Sightseeing

  • After breakfast, explore key attractions around Leh:
    • Shanti Stupa: A beautiful white-domed Buddhist stupa
    • Leh Palace: A historic palace offering panoramic views of Leh
    • Phyang Monastery: Known for its beautiful architecture and serene ambiance
    • Sangam: Witness the confluence of the Zanskar and Indus Rivers
    • Magnetic Hill: Experience the phenomenon of vehicles appearing to roll uphill
    • Gurudwara Pathar Sahib: A revered Sikh shrine
  • Return to your hotel for an overnight stay in Leh

Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley (160 km, 5-6 hours via Khardung La)

  • Depart by 8:00 AM, driving through the famous Khardung La Pass (5,359m), one of the highest motorable roads in the world
  • Arrive at Nubra Valley and check in at your homestay/camp
  • Visit:
    • Diskit Monastery: Famous for its 32-meter-tall Maitreya Buddha statue
    • Hunder Sand Dunes: A stunning cold desert landscape
    • Optional: Enjoy a camel ride on the unique double-humped Bactrian camels
  • Overnight stay in Nubra Valley

Day 4: Nubra Valley to Turtuk (80 km, 3 hours one way)

  • Start early at 7:30 AM for a scenic drive along the Shyok River to Turtuk, the northernmost village of India
  • Explore:
    • Baltistani culture and the unique heritage of the region
    • The historic Turtuk Mosque and Turtuk Monastery
    • A breathtaking viewpoint offering panoramic vistas
  • Return to Nubra by late afternoon
  • Overnight stay in Nubra Valley (Hunder)

Day 5: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake (via Shyok River)

  • After breakfast, embark on a picturesque drive to Pangong Lake via the scenic Shyok River route
  • Spend the afternoon by the stunning Pangong Tso, marveling at the lake's changing hues
  • Overnight stay in camps by the lake

Day 6: Pangong Lake to Leh (via Chang La Pass & Thiksey Monastery)

  • Begin your journey back to Leh, crossing the Chang La Pass, the third-highest motorable road in the world
  • En route, visit Thiksey Monastery: The largest monastery in Ladakh, known for its intricate architecture and peaceful ambiance
  • Arrive in Leh by evening
  • Overnight stay in Leh

Day 7: Departure

  • After breakfast, transfer to Leh Airport for your onward journey
  • Take with you cherished memories of Ladakh's unparalleled beauty
