Designed For Discovery

Take it from the experts, the Goa to Kerala route is one of India's most beautiful and picturesque trails one will ever come across. Lush mountains, winding rivers, coastal stretches and charming countryside make the road trip itself charming and fun.

The scenic route from Goa to Kerala offers breathtaking views

It begins with the vibrant energy in Goa and steadily moves into Kerala's serene green arms. With every passing kilometre, the landscaping seamlessly shifts, coconut trees replace concrete and the city dust gives way to fresh mountain air.

Right here, our carefully crafted Goa to Kerala tour package kicks in. You no longer need to figure out train timings or scramble for last-minute rooms. We plan everything to the T. All you need to do is show up, let go and immerse yourself in the incredible experience.

Monsoon Kerala Trip from Goa — A Rain-Soaked Dream

You might have been to Kerala before but if you haven't really seen Kerala during the monsoon, you haven't really seen it. From June to September, Kerala truly transforms to become God's own country.

Kerala's monsoon transforms the landscape into a lush paradise

The skies open up, rivers swell with life and the forests hum with deep rain-kissed music. Trekking through misty plantations, taking houseboat rides through the beautiful backwaters and sipping a hot cup of chai while watching the rains embrace the tiled rooftops becomes an unforgettable sensory experience.

Our monsoon Kerala trip from Goa is timed to show you this spectacular side of Kerala, the side locals love. You may also be lucky enough to witness waterfalls pouring in full-force, spice plantations glistening and monsoon festivals lighting up the villages. It's raw, mesmerizing and extraordinary.

Sample 7-Day Itinerary

Here's what your typical 7-day trip will look like:

Departure from Goa

Start your journey by road or train. Meet your fellow travelers, share your playlists, play travel games and get ready to have an adventure of a lifetime!

Arrival at Munnar

Reach the hills by morning. Munnar will welcome you with rolling tea gardens, cool breeze and peaceful trails. Visit local markets, unwind in your room and stretch your legs with a gentle evening hike.

Munnar Adventures

Trek to the Top Station for panoramic valley views. Stop by Eravikulam National Park where you might spot the Nilgiri Tahr. Explore waterfalls, echo points and the winding hill roads.

Thekkady Thrills

Leave for Thekkady early morning. This region near Periyar Tiger Reserve will give you a taste of Kerala's wilder side. Go for a guided jungle walk, bamboo rafting or elephant camp visit.

Alleppey/Kumarakom Backwaters

Check into a houseboat and sail away. The world will slow down as you float past coconut palms, waterbirds and quiet village life. Lunch will be served hot on deck.

Kochi City & Varkala Beach

Drive toward Kochi or Varkala. Kochi will charm you with colonial streets, quirky cafes and antique shops. Varkala offers cliffside cafes, golden beaches and good surf.

Return to Goa

It will be finally time to head back. Watch reels of your favourite moments, swap numbers and make a promise to travel again. You might return with a tan, new friends and stories for a lifetime.

Pro Tips for First-Timers

Keep an open mind

Every region will feel different. Embrace the diversity of experiences.

Carry a raincoat

Essential for monsoon trips to stay dry during sudden showers.

Use local transport

Canoes, jeeps, ferries add to the authentic experience.

Eat local

The authentic flavors will pleasantly surprise you.

Disconnect often

Mobile signal fades in remote spots. That's a gift!

Pack light

You'll be moving frequently - keep your luggage manageable.
