Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Kashmir experience

1

Arrival in Srinagar & Sightseeing

Our service starts with arrival at Srinagar airport. Meet & greet our representative. Transfer to Srinagar hotel – relax & freshen up. Leave for sightseeing trip to:

  • Mughal Gardens (Cheshmashahi, Nishat, Shalimar)
  • Asia's largest Tulip Garden (seasonal)
  • Shankaracharya Temple (hilltop panoramic views)

Back to hotel for dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar.

2

Srinagar to Sonamarg Excursion

Full day sightseeing trip to Sonamarg ("Meadow of Gold"). Enjoy a beautiful drive from Srinagar through picturesque landscapes.

  • Thajiwas Glacier (snow remains year-round)
  • Optional horse ride to glacier (at own cost)
  • Stunning views of Himalayan peaks

Return to Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

3

Srinagar to Gulmarg Excursion

Full day trip to Gulmarg, one of the most beautiful summer resorts in the valley.

  • Famous for its highest golf course and winter skiing
  • Optional Gondola ride to Apharwat Peak (13,000 ft, at own cost)
  • Check-in to deluxe houseboat on Dal Lake
  • Evening Shikara ride to enjoy sunset on Dal Lake

Dinner and overnight stay in houseboat.

4

Srinagar to Pahalgam

Scenic drive to Pahalgam with enroute visits:

  • Visit saffron fields (seasonal)
  • Avantipora ruins (ancient temple complex)

Check-in at Pahalgam hotel. Free time to enjoy the natural beauty or optional activities:

  • Horse riding to scenic spots
  • Local taxi to Aru Valley, Baisaran, Betaab Valley or Chandanwari (at own cost)

Dinner and overnight stay in Pahalgam.

5

Pahalgam to Srinagar

Return to Srinagar and check-in to deluxe houseboat on Dal Lake.

  • Leisure time to explore local markets
  • Evening Shikara ride to enjoy sunset on Dal Lake

Farewell dinner and overnight stay in houseboat.

6

Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, check out from houseboat.

  • Time permitting, shopping for souvenirs
  • Transfer to Srinagar airport for onward journey

Tour concludes with wonderful memories of Kashmir!
